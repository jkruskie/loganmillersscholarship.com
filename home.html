Logan Robert James Mills

Logan loved life.
His smile was contagious, bright, and beautiful.
From the time Logan was a little boy he always had an iron will and had begun to learn to focus his energy on his hopes and dreams.

Our Mission

Logan had a number of goals. On the top of the list, was loading up on college classes while in high school so he could try to beat his big sister in getting his bachelor’s before she did. His goal has weighed heavily on our hearts as he will not have the opportunity to try anymore. 

We aim to help someone else reach their goal by awarding $2,500 yearly to a local student in Logan’s honor. This award will remain until all funds have been depleted.
